Taxi Waala Cookie Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Taxi Waala! This Cookie Policy explains how Taxi Waala uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website, mobile application, or interact with our services online. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your browser or device by websites, applications, online media, and advertisements. They are widely used to make websites and applications work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information and assist with service or advertising personalization.

Why do we use Cookies?

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  1. Authentication: We use cookies to recognize you if you are logged in to our platform. This allows us to personalize your experience, such as showing you the correct information and options associated with your account.
  2. Security: We use cookies to support or enable security features to help keep our platform secure. For example, they help us to identify and prevent security risks.
  3. Preferences: We use cookies to remember your settings and preferences, such as your language preference and privacy settings.
  4. Analytics and Performance: We use cookies to collect information about how you interact with our platform and to help us improve it. For example, we may use cookies to determine which pages are visited most frequently, or to understand the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

Your Choices

You have the right to choose whether or not to accept cookies. However, please note that if you choose to refuse cookies, you may not be able to use the full functionality of our platform.

How to Manage Cookies

You can manage cookies through the settings of your browser or device. Please refer to your browser or device's help documentation for more information on how to manage or block cookies.

Changes to this Cookie Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. We encourage you to review this Cookie Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Cookie Policy, please contact us at taxiwaala@gmail.com.
